§ 02Follow-Up Rules
A.Wait 2 hours. Don't send immediately. Give them time to notice and reach out first. If no response after 2 hours, send the follow-up.
B.No guilt. Keep it light. "No worries — things come up." The goal is to reschedule, not to make them feel bad.
C.Offer 3 times. Always offer specific alternatives. "Let me know when works" puts the burden on them and reduces response rates.
D.Include calendar link. Make it easy to self-schedule. If you don't have a scheduling link, offer to send one.
E.Second no-show. If they miss twice, send a brief "Just checking in — is this still something you'd like to explore?" If no response, move on. Don't chase.

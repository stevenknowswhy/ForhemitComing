Voicemail Script
Retell AI & Live Voicemail
Internal Template

Trigger: Used when leaving voicemails for prospects, missed calls, and Retell AI voice agent responses

Duration: Keep under 20 seconds for Retell AI; under 30 seconds for live voicemail

§ 01Standard Voicemail (Live)

"Hi, this is {{callerName}} from Forhemit. I'm reaching out regarding {{context}}. I'd love to connect and share how we help business owners explore employee ownership transitions. Please call me back at (415) 555-0100, or I'll try you again soon. Thank you."

Key points: Say "employee ownership transitions" not "ESOP" on first contact. Keep it under 25 seconds. Always leave callback number.
§ 02Retell AI Script

"Thank you for calling Forhemit. We facilitate employee ownership transitions for business owners. I can help you schedule a consultation or answer basic questions about our services. How can I help you today?"

Retell AI Notes: Program to handle: (1) scheduling requests → book calendar, (2) general questions → provide overview, (3) urgent matters → transfer to live agent
§ 03Follow-Up Voicemail

"Hi, this is {{callerName}} from Forhemit again. I wanted to follow up on my previous message about employee ownership transitions. If you're interested in learning how this could work for your business, I'd be happy to schedule a brief call at your convenience. You can reach me at (415) 555-0100. Thank you."

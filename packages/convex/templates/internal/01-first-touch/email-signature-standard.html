§ 02Formatting Rules
Full name, no nicknames. Bold or regular weight — no italics.
Job title as listed in HR. No creative titles.
"Forhemit" — all caps FORHEMIT or standard case. No "Forhemit Stewardship Management Co." in the signature (too long).
Direct line or mobile. Format: (555) 123-4567. No extensions.
deals@forhemit.com — always use the shared inbox, not personal addresses.
forhemit.com — no https://, no www.
System default (Arial, Helvetica, or sans-serif). No custom fonts in email signatures.
Black text (#111 or #333). Company name in brass (#7A5C20) if your email client supports HTML.
§ 03What NOT to Include
Legal disclaimers are handled at the email gateway level, not in signatures.
Images in signatures are blocked by most email clients and increase spam scores.
LinkedIn, Twitter, etc. are not included. Forhemit's brand is the company, not individuals.
No inspirational quotes, mission statements, or taglines.
Promotional graphics are not permitted in individual signatures.
§ 04Variants
External emails: Use the standard signature above. Always include phone and website.
Internal emails: Name + title only. No contact info needed for internal threads.
Mobile replies: "Sent from my iPhone" or equivalent is acceptable for brief replies. Full signature required on first email in any thread.

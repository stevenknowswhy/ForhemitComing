§ 02Engagement Retainer — Stage 3
1.1 Confirm QofE-normalized TTM EBITDA from #96 QofE Findings Memo
Responsible: Deal Lead · Ref: #96
1.2 Prepare and send EBITDA Confirmation Letter (#119) to seller's CPA for counter-signature
Responsible: Deal Lead · Ref: #119
1.3 Populate Engagement Letter (#35) with correct tier, total fee, and fee schedule (Exhibit A)
Responsible: Deal Lead / Partner · Ref: #35, Fee Schedule
1.4 Mark Deal Stage = "Engaged" in CRM upon signed engagement letter
Responsible: Deal Admin · Ref: CRM
1.5 Generate Retainer invoice from template #95
Responsible: Finance / Deal Admin · Ref: #95, #90
1.6 Send invoice to company contact with wire instructions
Responsible: Deal Admin · Ref: Email
1.7 Log invoice in #94 Fee Collection Tracker
Responsible: Finance / Deal Admin · Ref: #94
1.8 Confirm wire receipt; update tracker to "Paid"
Responsible: Finance · Ref: #94, bank confirmation
1.9 If unpaid within 3 business days, send reminder and alert Deal Lead
Responsible: Finance / Deal Admin · Ref: #94 overdue alert
§ 03Validation Fee — Stage 4, Gate 1
2.1 Confirm Gate 1: Trustee FMV Adequacy Letter and LOI executed
Responsible: Deal Lead · Ref: #49, trustee letter, LOI
2.2 Generate Validation Fee invoice (#121), due net 7 days
Responsible: Finance / Deal Admin · Ref: #121 or #95
2.3 Send invoice to company contact referencing gate clearance
Responsible: Deal Admin · Ref: Email
2.4 Log in #94 Fee Collection Tracker; status "Pending"
Responsible: Finance · Ref: #94
2.5 Confirm payment receipt; update to "Paid"
Responsible: Finance · Ref: Bank, #94
2.6 Overdue: reminder at Day +3, call at Day +7, escalate to Deal Lead
Responsible: Finance / Deal Lead · Ref: #94 overdue flag
§ 04Commitment Fee — Stage 4, Gate 2
3.1 Confirm Gate 2: SBA Commitment Letter received and accepted
Responsible: Deal Lead · Ref: #50, SBA letter
3.2 Generate Commitment Fee invoice (#122), due net 7 days
Responsible: Finance / Deal Admin · Ref: #122
3.3 Send invoice with milestone note
Responsible: Deal Admin · Ref: Email
3.4 Log in #94 Fee Collection Tracker
Responsible: Finance · Ref: #94
3.5 Confirm payment; update tracker
Responsible: Finance · Ref: Bank, #94
3.6 Escalate overdue per §03 step 2.6 process
Responsible: Finance · Ref: Escalation matrix
§ 05Success Balance Pre-Authorization — Stage 4, Day ~90
4.1 Prepare Pre-Close Board Resolution — Fee Authorization (#120)
Responsible: Deal Lead / Legal · Ref: #120
4.2 Deliver resolution to company's board/manager for signature
Responsible: Deal Lead · Ref: Coordination
4.3 Obtain signed resolution; log in deal file
Responsible: Deal Admin · Ref: Deal file
4.4 Provide copy to trustee (part of closing package)
Responsible: Deal Lead · Ref: Trustee package
4.5 Update #94: mark Success Balance as "Authorized"
Responsible: Finance · Ref: #94
§ 06Success Balance Payment — Stage 5, Closing
5.1 At least 5 business days before closing, generate Success Balance invoice (#123)
Responsible: Finance / Deal Admin · Ref: #123
5.2 Send to company contact with wire instructions; must clear before ownership transfer
Responsible: Deal Admin · Ref: Email, #90
5.3 Log in #94 as "Pending — Due at Closing"
Responsible: Finance · Ref: #94
5.4 On closing day, verify wire receipt prior to closing call
Responsible: Finance / Deal Lead · Ref: Bank confirmation
5.5 Notify Deal Lead to proceed with closing
Responsible: Finance → Deal Lead · Ref: Internal
5.6 Update #94 to "Paid"; record closing date
Responsible: Finance · Ref: #94
5.7 If wire not received by closing morning, pause closing and alert all parties
Responsible: Deal Lead · Ref: Crisis protocol
§ 07Annual Stewardship Fee — Stage 6, Day 121+
6.1 No later than 10 days post-close, prepare Stewardship Payment Schedule (#124)
Responsible: Finance / COOP Lead · Ref: #124
6.2 Send payment schedule to CEO and trustee with Summary Page (#126)
Responsible: Deal Lead / COOP Lead · Ref: Cover email
6.3 If new CEO is onboarded later, deliver Officer Onboarding Package (#127)
Responsible: COOP Lead · Ref: #127
6.4 Set up recurring tasks/invoices for each tranche (#125)
Responsible: Finance / COOP Admin · Ref: #125, task system
6.5 For each tranche: generate invoice, send, log in #94, track payment
Responsible: Finance · Ref: #94
6.6 Payment late beyond 15 days: notify COOP Lead and trustee
Responsible: Finance → COOP Lead · Ref: Escalation matrix
§ 08Ongoing — Tracker Maintenance
7.1 Review #94 for all active deals; flag overdue invoices
Responsible: Finance · Frequency: Weekly
7.2 Reconcile fee receipts with bank statements
Responsible: Finance · Frequency: Monthly
7.3 Report fee status in weekly internal review (#93)
Responsible: Deal Lead · Frequency: Weekly
7.4 Archive fee records at deal/stewardship completion
Responsible: Finance / Deal Admin · Ref: At completion

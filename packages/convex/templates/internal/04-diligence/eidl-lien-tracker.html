§ 03Payoff / Release Options
A.Full payoff. Pay the EIDL balance in full at or before closing. SBA releases UCC-1 within 30 days of payoff. Cleanest option — no lien at closing.
B.SBA subordination. Request SBA to subordinate the EIDL lien to the new SBA 7(a) loan. Requires SBA approval. Processing time: 30–60 days. The EIDL remains in place but is junior to the 7(a) loan.
C.SBA consent to transfer. Request SBA consent to transfer the EIDL obligation to the post-ESOP entity. The C-Corp assumes the EIDL debt. Requires SBA approval and may affect 7(a) eligibility.

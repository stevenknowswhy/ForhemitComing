§ 01Decision Tree

Work through each decision point sequentially. A single disqualifying answer at any gate sends the deal to the indicated outcome. All criteria must pass to reach Qualified status.

Gate 1 — EBITDA Threshold
Does the company have trailing 12-month EBITDA of $1,000,000 or greater?
YES
Proceed to Gate 2
NO
EBITDA below threshold. Route to Decline — notify broker with standard no-fit response.
Gate 2 — Ownership Concentration
Is there a single majority owner (or aligned ownership group) willing to sell 100%?
YES
Proceed to Gate 3
NO
Complex ownership. Route to Conditional — requires deal structuring review before proceeding.
Gate 3 — Industry Fit
Is the company in a service, professional services, light manufacturing, or distribution industry? Does it avoid heavily regulated sectors (cannabis, firearms, gambling)?
YES
Proceed to Gate 4
NO
Industry outside criteria. Route to Decline — unless compelling offset exists (review case-by-case).
Gate 4 — Geographic Location
Is the company headquartered in the United States with operations that do not require significant international presence?
YES
Proceed to Gate 5
NO
Location outside coverage. Route to Decline.
Gate 5 — Management Depth
Does the company have a management team of 3+ people (excluding the owner) who can operate the business post-close?
YES
Proceed to Gate 6
NO
Thin management. Route to Conditional — COOP succession plan required before engagement.
Gate 6 — Seller Motivation
Is the seller motivated to transact within 12 months and willing to accept a structured deal (seller note, earnout, or deferred compensation)?
YES
QUALIFIED. Route to deal team for Pre-Flight Checklist and engagement.
NO
Unrealistic expectations. Route to Conditional — nurture relationship, revisit in 6 months.
§ 02Classification Outcomes
●QUALIFIED — All gates passed. Issue Pre-Flight Checklist. Assign deal lead. Schedule seller call within 3 business days.
●CONDITIONAL — One or more soft gates failed. Requires internal review before proceeding. Assign analyst to prepare brief. Decision within 5 business days.
●DECLINE — Hard gate failed. Send appropriate decline response (broker or seller). Log in CRM. No further action unless circumstances change.

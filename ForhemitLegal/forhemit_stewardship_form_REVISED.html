Upon termination for cause, all unpaid fees become immediately due. Upon termination for convenience, Company pays prorated fees through termination date.

The Company shall indemnify Forhemit from claims arising from the Company's breach, operations, or business (except from Forhemit's gross negligence or willful misconduct). 11 Dispute Resolution Incomplete ▾

Disputes shall first be negotiated between principal representatives. If unresolved within 15 days, the dispute escalates to executive leadership. If still unresolved after 30 days, parties proceed to mediation, then binding AAA arbitration.

The parties hereby waive any right to trial by jury. The prevailing party in any dispute shall be entitled to recover reasonable attorney fees, costs, and expenses.

## 1. PARTIES AND NATURE OF ENGAGEMENT

**1.1 Parties.** This Agreement is between Forhemit Transition Stewardship, a California Public Benefit Corporation ("Forhemit"), and [Company Name] ("Company"), acting through [Authorized Officer Name], [Authorized Officer Title] ("Authorized Officer").

**1.2 Nature of Services.** Forhemit is a transaction stewardship firm. Forhemit facilitates employee ownership transitions — coordinating the transaction team, managing the process timeline, preparing the lender and trustee package, and providing post-close operational stewardship.

**IMPORTANT: FORHEMIT IS NOT:**
- A buyer, investor, or lender in any transaction involving the Company
- A broker, finder, or investment banker requiring registration under federal or state securities laws
- Legal counsel, tax advisor, or financial advisor providing investment, tax, or legal advice

**1.3 ERISA DISCLAIMER.** Forhemit is NOT an ERISA fiduciary and does NOT provide ERISA legal advice. All matters relating to ESOP plan qualification, employee eligibility, prohibited transactions under ERISA Section 406, fiduciary duties under ERISA, and ERISA Section 408(b)(2) disclosures must be reviewed by independent ERISA counsel qualified in employee ownership transitions. Company represents that it has retained or will retain such independent ERISA counsel prior to signing any Letter of Intent or definitive transaction agreement.

**1.4 TAX ADVICE DISCLAIMER.** Forhemit does NOT provide tax, accounting, or financial advisory services. All tax matters — including but not limited to entity structure, §1042 election eligibility, Qualified Replacement Property reinvestment strategies, SBA loan tax implications, ESOP plan qualification, and corporate tax consequences — MUST be reviewed by independent qualified tax professionals (CPA and/or tax counsel) licensed in the Company's jurisdiction.

Forhemit makes NO representations or warranties regarding any tax consequences of any transaction structure. Company acknowledges that any discussion of capital structure, debt/equity allocation, or financing alternatives by Forhemit is limited to general operational guidance only and does NOT constitute tax advice.

**1.5 Fee Payment Responsibility.** Forhemit does not appear on the closing statement. Forhemit's fees do not reduce seller proceeds. Forhemit's engagement does not affect broker commission. All Forhemit fees are paid by the Company as operating business expenses.

### SECTION 1042 ROLLOVER ACKNOWLEDGMENT

**NOTICE:** Section 1042 of the Internal Revenue Code may allow qualifying sellers to defer capital gains tax by reinvesting sale proceeds in Qualified Replacement Property (QRP). HOWEVER, this election is ONLY available if the selling entity is a C-Corporation at the time of sale. S-Corporations DO NOT qualify.

**If the Company is currently taxed as an S-Corporation and wishes to access §1042 deferral, conversion to C-Corporation status is required BEFORE the sale. This conversion has PERMANENT tax consequences:**

1. **DOUBLE TAXATION:** All future earnings will be subject to corporate-level tax and shareholder-level tax
2. **BUILT-IN GAINS TAX:** May apply for up to five years after conversion
3. **LOSS OF PASS-THROUGH:** Losses, deductions, and credits will no longer pass through to shareholders
4. **STATE TAX IMPACT:** State-level tax treatment will change
5. **IRREVERSIBLE:** Conversion from S-Corporation to C-Corporation is permanent

This warning is provided for informational purposes only. Forhemit does NOT provide tax advice and makes NO recommendation regarding §1042 election or S-Corporation conversion. Company MUST consult with qualified tax counsel BEFORE executing any Letter of Intent.
